# ADA Standards of Medical Care in Diabetes Reference

## Glycemic Targets

| Population | A1c Target | Rationale |
|-----------|-----------|-----------|
| Most nonpregnant adults | <7.0% | Reduces microvascular complications |
| Selected individuals (short diabetes duration, no CVD, long life expectancy) | <6.5% | If achievable without significant hypoglycemia |
| Older adults (complex/intermediate health) | <8.0% | Avoid hypoglycemia, limited benefit of tight control |
| Older adults (very complex/poor health) | <8.5% | Focus on avoiding hyperglycemic symptoms |
| Pregnancy (pre-existing) | <6.5% | Optimal: <6.0% without hypoglycemia |
| Gestational diabetes | Fasting <95, 1-hr <140, 2-hr <120 mg/dL | Per ADA/ACOG |

## A1c Monitoring Schedule

| Situation | Frequency |
|-----------|-----------|
| At or above target, stable regimen | Every 6 months |
| Not at target or therapy change | Every 3 months |
| New diagnosis or medication change | 3 months after change |

## Diabetes Diagnosis Criteria

| Test | Diabetes | Prediabetes | Normal |
|------|----------|-------------|--------|
| A1c | >=6.5% | 5.7-6.4% | <5.7% |
| Fasting glucose | >=126 mg/dL | 100-125 mg/dL | <100 mg/dL |
| 2-hr OGTT | >=200 mg/dL | 140-199 mg/dL | <140 mg/dL |
| Random glucose + symptoms | >=200 mg/dL | -- | -- |

## Annual Monitoring Requirements

| Test | LOINC | Frequency | Goal |
|------|-------|-----------|------|
| HbA1c | 4548-4 | Every 3-6 months | Per individual target |
| Fasting lipid panel | 57698-3 | Annually (more if abnormal) | LDL <100 (no ASCVD), <70 (ASCVD) |
| Serum creatinine/eGFR | 2160-0 / 33914-3 | Annually | Monitor for CKD |
| Urine albumin/Cr ratio | 14959-1 | Annually (Type 2: at diagnosis; Type 1: 5 years after diagnosis) | <30 mg/g |
| Comprehensive metabolic panel | 24323-8 | Annually | Electrolytes, liver, renal function |
| TSH | 3016-3 | At diagnosis (Type 1), periodically | 0.4-4.0 mIU/L |

## Lipid Goals in Diabetes

| Risk Category | LDL Goal | Statin Intensity |
|--------------|----------|-----------------|
| Age 40-75, no ASCVD, no risk factors | <100 mg/dL | Moderate-intensity |
| Age 40-75, with ASCVD risk factors | <100 mg/dL | High-intensity |
| Established ASCVD | <70 mg/dL | High-intensity |
| Established ASCVD, on max statin, LDL >70 | <55 mg/dL | Add ezetimibe, then PCSK9i |
| Age 20-39 with risk factors | Consider statin | Moderate-intensity |
| Age >75 | Continue if tolerated | Based on benefit/risk |

## Blood Pressure Goals

| Population | Target |
|-----------|--------|
| Most adults with diabetes | <130/80 mmHg |
| Higher risk for adverse drug effects | <140/90 mmHg |
| Pregnancy with chronic HTN | <135/85 mmHg |

## Diabetic Kidney Disease (DKD) Staging

### Albuminuria Categories
| Category | UACR (mg/g) | Term |
|----------|-------------|------|
| A1 | <30 | Normal to mildly increased |
| A2 | 30-300 | Moderately increased (microalbuminuria) |
| A3 | >300 | Severely increased (macroalbuminuria) |

### eGFR Categories
| Stage | eGFR (mL/min/1.73m2) | Description |
|-------|---------------------|-------------|
| G1 | >=90 | Normal or high |
| G2 | 60-89 | Mildly decreased |
| G3a | 45-59 | Mildly to moderately decreased |
| G3b | 30-44 | Moderately to severely decreased |
| G4 | 15-29 | Severely decreased |
| G5 | <15 | Kidney failure |

## Medication Therapy Stepwise Approach (Type 2)

1. **First-line**: Metformin + lifestyle modifications
2. **If A1c above target after 3 months**: Add second agent based on patient factors:
   - ASCVD or high CV risk: GLP-1 RA or SGLT2 inhibitor (proven CV benefit)
   - Heart failure: SGLT2 inhibitor (proven HF benefit)
   - CKD: SGLT2 inhibitor (proven renal benefit) or GLP-1 RA
   - Need to minimize hypoglycemia: GLP-1 RA, SGLT2i, DPP-4i, TZD
   - Need to minimize weight gain: GLP-1 RA, SGLT2i
   - Cost is major factor: Sulfonylurea, TZD
3. **If still above target**: Add third agent or insulin
4. **If A1c >10% or symptomatic hyperglycemia**: Consider starting insulin early
