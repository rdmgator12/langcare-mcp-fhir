# CAP/CLIA Critical Value Thresholds

## Chemistry Critical Values

| Analyte | LOINC | Critical Low | Critical High | Immediate Action |
|---------|-------|-------------|--------------|------------------|
| Sodium | 2951-2 | <120 mEq/L | >160 mEq/L | Low: assess volume, serum osm, correct no faster than 8 mEq/L/24h. High: free water replacement |
| Potassium | 2823-3 | <2.5 mEq/L | >6.5 mEq/L | Low: IV KCl, ECG, Mg check. High: ECG, calcium gluconate, insulin/glucose, kayexalate |
| Calcium (total) | 17861-6 | <6.0 mg/dL | >13.0 mg/dL | Low: IV calcium gluconate. High: NS hydration, calcitonin, bisphosphonate |
| Calcium (ionized) | 1994-3 | <0.78 mmol/L | >1.58 mmol/L | Low: IV calcium. High: hydration, diuresis |
| Glucose | 2345-7 | <40 mg/dL | >500 mg/dL | Low: D50 IV or oral glucose. High: insulin, fluid resuscitation, DKA/HHS protocol |
| Magnesium | 19123-9 | <1.0 mg/dL | >4.7 mg/dL | Low: IV Mg replacement. High: hold Mg-containing meds, calcium gluconate |
| Phosphate | 2777-1 | <1.0 mg/dL | >8.9 mg/dL | Low: IV phosphate. High: hydration, phosphate binders |
| Creatinine | 2160-0 | -- | >10.0 mg/dL | Nephrology consult, dialysis evaluation |
| BUN | 3094-0 | -- | >100 mg/dL | Evaluate for uremia, dialysis consideration |
| Total Bilirubin | 1975-2 | -- | >15.0 mg/dL (adult), >20.0 (neonate) | Evaluate hepatic failure vs hemolysis. Neonate: phototherapy/exchange |
| Lactate | 2524-7 | -- | >4.0 mmol/L | Evaluate for shock/sepsis, fluid resuscitation |
| Ammonia | 1742-6 | -- | >100 umol/L | Lactulose, rifaximin, evaluate hepatic encephalopathy |

## Hematology Critical Values

| Analyte | LOINC | Critical Low | Critical High | Immediate Action |
|---------|-------|-------------|--------------|------------------|
| Hemoglobin | 718-7 | <7.0 g/dL | >20.0 g/dL | Low: transfusion, type/screen. High: phlebotomy, evaluate polycythemia |
| Hematocrit | 4544-3 | <20% | >60% | Low: transfusion. High: phlebotomy |
| WBC | 26464-8 | <2.0 x10^3/uL | >30.0 x10^3/uL | Low: neutropenic precautions, evaluate cause. High: evaluate leukocytosis cause |
| Platelets | 777-3 | <20 x10^3/uL | >1000 x10^3/uL | Low: platelet transfusion, bleeding precautions. High: evaluate thrombocytosis |
| Neutrophils (ANC) | 751-8 | <500/uL | -- | Neutropenic precautions, evaluate for febrile neutropenia |
| Fibrinogen | 3255-7 | <100 mg/dL | -- | Cryoprecipitate, evaluate for DIC |

## Coagulation Critical Values

| Analyte | LOINC | Critical Low | Critical High | Immediate Action |
|---------|-------|-------------|--------------|------------------|
| PT | 5902-2 | -- | >30 seconds | Hold anticoagulant, vitamin K consideration |
| INR | 6301-6 | -- | >5.0 | Hold warfarin, vitamin K (PO if INR 5-9, IV if >9 or bleeding) |
| aPTT | 3173-2 | -- | >100 seconds | Hold heparin, protamine consideration if bleeding |
| D-dimer | 48066-5 | -- | >4.0 mcg/mL FEU | Evaluate for DIC, PE, DVT |

## Cardiac Markers

| Analyte | LOINC | Threshold | Immediate Action |
|---------|-------|-----------|------------------|
| Troponin I | 10839-9 | >0.04 ng/mL | ACS protocol, cardiology consult, serial troponins |
| Troponin I (HS) | 89579-7 | >26 ng/L (M), >16 ng/L (F) | Serial troponins, evaluate for NSTEMI |
| BNP | 42637-9 | >500 pg/mL | Evaluate for acute heart failure |

## Blood Gas Critical Values

| Analyte | LOINC | Critical Low | Critical High | Immediate Action |
|---------|-------|-------------|--------------|------------------|
| pH (arterial) | 2744-1 | <7.20 | >7.60 | Evaluate cause, respiratory vs metabolic |
| pCO2 | 2019-8 | <20 mmHg | >70 mmHg | High: ventilation support. Low: evaluate hyperventilation cause |
| pO2 | 2703-7 | <40 mmHg | -- | Supplemental O2, evaluate respiratory failure |

## Toxicology/Drug Levels

| Analyte | LOINC | Critical High | Immediate Action |
|---------|-------|--------------|------------------|
| Digoxin | 10535-3 | >2.0 ng/mL | Hold digoxin, Digibind if severe toxicity |
| Lithium | 14334-7 | >1.5 mEq/L | Hold lithium, hydration, dialysis if >4.0 |
| Theophylline | 4049-3 | >20 mcg/mL | Hold theophylline, monitor rhythm |
| Phenytoin | 3968-5 | >30 mcg/mL | Hold phenytoin, monitor for toxicity |
| Valproic acid | 4086-5 | >150 mcg/mL | Hold valproate, L-carnitine |
| Acetaminophen | 3298-8 | >150 mcg/mL (4hr) | N-acetylcysteine protocol |
| Salicylate | 4024-6 | >40 mg/dL | Alkalinization, hemodialysis if >100 |
| Ethanol | 5643-2 | >300 mg/dL | Supportive care, monitor respiratory status |

## Reporting Requirements
- CAP requires notification within **1 hour** of result verification
- Notification must include: patient identifier, test name, result, critical threshold, date/time
- Read-back confirmation required from receiving clinician
- Document: who was notified, time of notification, read-back confirmation
