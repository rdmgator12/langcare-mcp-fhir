## Look-Alike/Sound-Alike (LASA) Pairs

| Drug A | Drug B | Risk |
|--------|--------|------|
| HumaLOG (lispro) | HumuLIN (regular/NPH) | Insulin type confusion |
| Hydroxyzine | Hydralazine | Antihistamine vs antihypertensive |
| Metformin | Metronidazole | Diabetes drug vs antibiotic |
| Clonidine | Clonazepam | Antihypertensive vs benzodiazepine |
| Prednisone | Prednisolone | Different bioavailability |
| Tramadol | Trazodone | Opioid vs antidepressant |
| Bupropion | Buspirone | Antidepressant vs anxiolytic |
