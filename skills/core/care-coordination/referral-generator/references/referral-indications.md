### Urgency Classification Key
- **STAT**: Same-day or next-day evaluation required. Life-threatening or rapidly progressive.
- **Urgent**: Within 1-2 weeks. Significant symptoms or risk of deterioration.
- **Routine**: Within 4-6 weeks. Stable condition, optimization or screening.
