# Standard Follow-Up Intervals

## Lab Re-Check Intervals for Abnormal Results

### Critical Values (Immediate / Same-Day Re-Check)

| Lab | Critical Value | Action | Re-Check Interval |
|-----|---------------|--------|-------------------|
| Potassium | < 2.5 or > 6.5 mEq/L | Immediate treatment, continuous monitoring | Same day after correction |
| Sodium | < 120 or > 160 mEq/L | Urgent treatment | Every 4-6 hours during correction |
| Glucose | < 40 or > 600 mg/dL | Immediate treatment | Every 1-2 hours until stable |
| Hemoglobin | < 7.0 g/dL | Transfusion consideration | Post-transfusion (within hours) |
| Platelets | < 20,000/uL | Transfusion, hematology consult | Post-transfusion, then daily |
| WBC | < 1,500/uL (neutropenic) | Infection precautions, consider G-CSF | Daily |
| INR | > 5.0 (on warfarin) | Hold warfarin, consider vitamin K | Next day |
| Troponin | Positive / elevated | Cardiology consult, serial monitoring | Every 3-6 hours x3 |
| Lactate | > 4.0 mmol/L | Sepsis workup, fluid resuscitation | Every 2-4 hours |
| Calcium (corrected) | > 14.0 mg/dL | IV fluids, calcitonin, bisphosphonate | Every 6-12 hours |

### Significant Abnormal Values (Re-Check Within 1-2 Weeks)

| Lab | Abnormal Value | LOINC | Re-Check Interval | Notes |
|-----|---------------|-------|-------------------|-------|
| Potassium | 5.1-6.0 mEq/L | 2823-3 | 3-7 days | Review medications (ACE/ARB, spironolactone, K+ supplements) |
| Potassium | 3.0-3.4 mEq/L | 2823-3 | 3-7 days | Supplement and recheck |
| Sodium | 125-129 mEq/L | 2951-2 | 3-7 days | Fluid restriction, evaluate cause |
| Creatinine | > 50% increase from baseline | 2160-0 | 3-7 days | Evaluate for AKI, hold nephrotoxic meds |
| Hemoglobin | 7.0-9.0 g/dL (new) | 718-7 | 1-2 weeks | Iron studies, reticulocyte count |
| WBC | 1,500-3,000/uL | 6690-2 | 1 week | Repeat with differential |
| WBC | > 20,000/uL | 6690-2 | 3-7 days | Evaluate for infection, malignancy |
| AST or ALT | > 5x ULN | 1920-8/1742-6 | 3-7 days | Hepatic workup |
| TSH | > 10 mIU/L | 3016-3 | 6-8 weeks (after starting treatment) | Free T4, start levothyroxine |
| TSH | < 0.1 mIU/L | 3016-3 | 4-6 weeks (after starting treatment) | Free T4, free T3, evaluate cause |
| HbA1c | > 9% | 4548-4 | 3 months | Medication adjustment, diabetes education |
| BNP | > 400 pg/mL | 42637-9 | 2-4 weeks | Heart failure management |

### Mildly Abnormal Values (Re-Check Within 1-3 Months)

| Lab | Abnormal Value | LOINC | Re-Check Interval | Notes |
|-----|---------------|-------|-------------------|-------|
| HbA1c | 6.5-9.0% | 4548-4 | 3 months | On stable treatment |
| LDL | > 130 mg/dL | 18262-6 | 6-8 weeks (after starting statin) | Fasting lipid panel |
| LDL | > 100 mg/dL (with CVD) | 18262-6 | 6-8 weeks (after adjustment) | May need higher-intensity statin |
| TSH | 5.0-10.0 mIU/L | 3016-3 | 6-8 weeks | Subclinical hypothyroidism, repeat to confirm |
| Vitamin D | < 20 ng/mL | 1989-3 | 3 months (after supplementation) | Supplement with D3 |
| Ferritin | < 30 ng/mL | 2276-4 | 3 months (after iron therapy) | |
| eGFR | 30-59 mL/min (stable) | 33914-3 | 3-6 months | CKD monitoring |
| Urine albumin/creatinine | 30-300 mg/g | 9318-7 | 3-6 months | Repeat to confirm, start ACE/ARB |
| PSA | 4.0-10.0 ng/mL | 2857-1 | 6-12 weeks | Repeat, consider urology referral |
| Hemoglobin | 9.0-11.0 g/dL (chronic) | 718-7 | 1-3 months | Monitor for worsening |

## Post-Procedure Follow-Up Requirements

### Surgical Procedures

| Procedure | First Follow-Up | Subsequent Follow-Up | Key Assessments |
|-----------|----------------|---------------------|-----------------|
| Major abdominal surgery | 2 weeks | 6 weeks, then per condition | Wound healing, drain removal, pathology |
| Laparoscopic surgery | 1-2 weeks | 4-6 weeks | Port site healing, activity advancement |
| Joint replacement | 2 weeks | 6 weeks, 3 months, 1 year | Wound, ROM, PT progress, X-ray |
| Cardiac surgery (CABG) | 2 weeks | 6 weeks, 3 months | Sternotomy healing, cardiac rehab, lipids |
| Spine surgery | 2 weeks | 6 weeks, 3 months | Wound, neurologic exam, imaging |
| Hernia repair | 2 weeks | 6 weeks | Wound, activity advancement |
| Thyroidectomy | 1-2 weeks | 6 weeks (TSH check) | Calcium, voice, TSH |
| Appendectomy | 1-2 weeks | As needed | Wound healing |
| Cholecystectomy | 1-2 weeks | As needed | Wound, diet tolerance |

### Endoscopic Procedures

| Procedure | Follow-Up | Key Actions |
|-----------|-----------|-------------|
| Colonoscopy (normal) | Based on screening interval | 10-year if average risk |
| Colonoscopy (polyps found) | Per pathology (see below) | Surveillance colonoscopy scheduling |
| EGD with biopsy | 1-2 weeks for pathology results | H. pylori treatment if positive |
| ERCP | 24-48 hours post-procedure call | Watch for pancreatitis symptoms |
| Bronchoscopy with biopsy | 1-2 weeks for pathology results | Pneumothorax symptoms in 24 hours |

### Post-Polypectomy Colonoscopy Surveillance

| Finding | Next Colonoscopy | SNOMED/CPT |
|---------|-----------------|------------|
| 1-2 tubular adenomas < 10mm | 7-10 years | Follow-up |
| 3-4 tubular adenomas < 10mm | 3-5 years | Follow-up |
| 5-10 adenomas | 3 years | Follow-up |
| Adenoma >= 10mm | 3 years | Follow-up |
| Adenoma with villous histology | 3 years | Follow-up |
| Adenoma with high-grade dysplasia | 3 years | Follow-up |
| Sessile serrated polyp < 10mm, no dysplasia | 5 years | Follow-up |
| Sessile serrated polyp >= 10mm or with dysplasia | 3 years | Follow-up |
| Piecemeal resection >= 20mm | 6 months | Completion assessment |
| Hyperplastic polyps (rectosigmoid only) | 10 years | Average risk interval |

### Cardiac Procedures

| Procedure | First Follow-Up | Labs/Tests | Long-Term |
|-----------|----------------|-----------|-----------|
| PCI with stent | 2-4 weeks | Lipids, CBC | DAPT duration per stent type |
| Pacemaker implant | 1 week (wound), 1 month (device check) | Device interrogation | Every 6-12 months |
| ICD implant | 1 week, 1 month | Device interrogation | Every 3-6 months |
| Ablation (AF) | 1 month, 3 months | ECG, rhythm monitoring | Per EP protocol |
| Cardiac catheterization (diagnostic) | 1-2 weeks | Discuss results, plan | Per findings |
| TAVR | 1 month, 6 months, 1 year | Echo, labs | Annual echo |

### Biopsy Results

| Biopsy Type | Result Timeline | Follow-Up Action |
|-------------|----------------|------------------|
| Skin biopsy | 7-10 days | Review pathology, determine if excision needed |
| Breast biopsy | 5-7 days | Pathology review, multidisciplinary conference if malignant |
| Prostate biopsy | 7-10 days | Pathology review, Gleason grading, staging if cancer |
| Bone marrow biopsy | 7-14 days | Hematology review, flow cytometry, genetics |
| Liver biopsy | 7-10 days | Hepatology review, staging if fibrosis |
| Renal biopsy | 5-7 days | Nephrology review, treatment plan |
| Lymph node biopsy | 7-10 days | Pathology, oncology if malignant |

## Chronic Disease Management Intervals

### Diabetes Mellitus

| Task | Interval | LOINC | Notes |
|------|----------|-------|-------|
| HbA1c | Every 3 months (if not at goal), every 6 months (at goal) | 4548-4 | Goal typically < 7% |
| Fasting lipid panel | Annual | 57698-3 | More often if on new statin |
| Urine albumin/creatinine | Annual | 9318-7 | Nephropathy screening |
| Serum creatinine/eGFR | Annual (or per CKD stage) | 2160-0/33914-3 | |
| Dilated eye exam | Annual (biennial if no retinopathy x2) | -- | Ophthalmology referral |
| Foot exam | Annual (more often if neuropathy) | -- | Monofilament testing |
| Blood pressure | Every visit | 85354-9 | Goal < 130/80 |
| Hepatitis B vaccination | If not immune, age 19-59 | -- | |

### Heart Failure

| Task | Interval | LOINC | Notes |
|------|----------|-------|-------|
| BNP/NT-proBNP | Per clinical status, at every med change | 42637-9/33762-6 | Trending |
| BMP (K, Cr, Na) | 1-2 weeks after med change, then q3-6 months | -- | ACE/ARB/ARNI, diuretics, spironolactone |
| CBC | Every 6-12 months | 58410-2 | Anemia monitoring |
| Echocardiogram | Annual or with clinical change | -- | EF trending |
| Daily weight | Daily (patient self-monitoring) | 29463-7 | Report > 2 lb/day or > 5 lb/week gain |
| Iron studies | Annual | 2276-4 | If HFrEF, consider IV iron if deficient |
| Volume assessment | Every visit | -- | JVD, edema, lung sounds |

### Hypertension

| Task | Interval | LOINC | Notes |
|------|----------|-------|-------|

### CKD (Chronic Kidney Disease)

| CKD Stage | eGFR Range | Monitoring Interval | Additional Labs |
|-----------|-----------|--------------------|-----------------|
| Stage 1-2 | >= 60 | Annual | UACR, BP |
| Stage 3a | 45-59 | Every 6 months | UACR, Ca, Phos, PTH, bicarb, CBC |
| Stage 3b | 30-44 | Every 3-6 months | Same as 3a + refer to nephrology |
| Stage 4 | 15-29 | Every 3 months | Same + AV fistula discussion |
| Stage 5 | < 15 | Every 1-3 months | Same + dialysis/transplant planning |

## Medication-Specific Monitoring

### New Medication Monitoring Labs

| Medication Class | Baseline Labs | First Re-Check | Ongoing | Key Concern |
|-----------------|---------------|----------------|---------|-------------|
| ACE inhibitor / ARB | BMP | 1-2 weeks | 3-6 months | Hyperkalemia, AKI |
| Statin | Lipids, hepatic panel | 6-8 weeks (lipids) | Annual | LFTs only if symptoms |
| Metformin | BMP, B12 | 3 months | Annual | Lactic acidosis (rare), B12 deficiency |
| SGLT2 inhibitor | BMP, A1c | 3 months | 3-6 months | Euglycemic DKA, UTI, genital mycotic |
| Thiazide diuretic | BMP | 1-2 weeks | 3-6 months | Hyponatremia, hypokalemia |
| Loop diuretic | BMP | 1 week | Per clinical status | Hypokalemia, AKI, volume depletion |
| Spironolactone | BMP | 1 week | 1 month, then q3 months | Hyperkalemia |
| Lithium | Lithium level, BMP, TSH, Ca | 5-7 days | q3 months (level), q6 months (TSH/renal) | Toxicity, hypothyroidism, renal |
| Clozapine | CBC with ANC | Weekly x6 months | Every 2 weeks x6 months, then monthly | Agranulocytosis |
| Methotrexate | CBC, LFTs, Cr | 2-4 weeks | Every 1-3 months | Pancytopenia, hepatotoxicity |
| Amiodarone | TFTs, LFTs, PFTs, CXR | 3 months | Every 6 months | Thyroid, pulmonary, hepatic toxicity |
| Allopurinol | Uric acid, BMP | 2-4 weeks | q3-6 months until at goal | Target uric acid < 6.0 |
| Carbamazepine | CBC, LFTs, Na | 2-4 weeks | Every 3-6 months | Hyponatremia, agranulocytosis |
| Valproic acid | Level, CBC, LFTs | 1-2 weeks | Every 3-6 months | Hepatotoxicity, thrombocytopenia |
